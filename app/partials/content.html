Who we are

The Richard Ivey School of Business is proud to be hosting its first every Model United Nations on March 12th, 2016. Founded in 1923, the Richard Ivey School of Business is Canada’s premier undergraduate program (HBA) and is one of the oldest business programs in the country. Bloomberg Business week’s 2015 has ranked Ivey’s full time MBA program 1st internationally, and our graduates are world class leaders who “Think Globally, Act Strategically, and Contribute to Societies in Which They Operate”.

Who Can Attend?

IVEYMUN will be open to both Western and non-Western students. No experience is necessary.

Registration

From the Chairs of IveyMUN

Hey everyone,

Ivey's Model United Nations team will be holding our first annual conference on March 12th from 9:30 AM until 6:30 PM. IVEYMUN will be open to both Western and non-Western students. No experience is necessary.

The committee fee is $20. You can register online or in person. To register online please click here. If you prefer to pay in person, we will also be holding a registration table in the basement of the Ivey Building (near the HBAA office) from February 22-March 3 from 1-2 pm each day.

The deadline to register and pay the committee fee is March 3.

Once delegates are registered, we will reach out to you through email for your committee selection.

For inquiries, please contact: info@iveymun.com

We will be hosting the following Committees:

  • Disarmament and International Security (DISEC)
  • The Ides of March, 44 B.C. Rome (Crisis)
  • Assad's Syrian Cabinet (Crisis)
  • The Zombie Apocalypse, 2021 (Advanced Crisis)
