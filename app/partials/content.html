Registration

The deadline to register and pay the committee fee is March 3rd.

The committee fee is $20. You can register online or in person. To register online, please do so here.

If you prefer to pay in person, we will also be holding a registration table in the basement of the Ivey Building (near the HBAA office) from February 24-March 3 from 1-2 pm each day.
